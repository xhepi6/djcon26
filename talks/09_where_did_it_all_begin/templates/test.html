What you're looking at

atomic() problem: every nested atomic() creates a SAVEPOINT + RELEASE SAVEPOINT pair, even when you don't need partial rollback. At Kraken's scale (16M lines of Python), this adds up.

subatomic solution: split atomic() into db.transaction(), db.transaction_required(), db.savepoint(), db.durable(). Each says exactly what it does. No surprise savepoints.
